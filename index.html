Six Realms

In Buddhist cosmology, they speak of the six realms of existence and rebirth. These six realms can also be seen as six types of mind states or psycho-emotional worlds where transmigration is understood as shifts from one psycho-emotional formation to another. Indeed, in this sense, one may even migrate through the different worlds within a single day, within a couple of minutes, perhaps even seconds, depending on momentary turbulence of your life or one may as well be stuck in one of these worls for years, decades and aeons.
